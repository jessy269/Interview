Interview with
Ethan Arsenault
by Jessy McKee

Why do you want to design?

Because it is fun to create stuff and experiment in many different styles and mediums while being artistically expressive and providing my own spin on things.

What is difficult or frustrating for you within design and how do you work to push past that?

It is always challenging to be creative and trying to stand out with your work, I push past that by having a clear focus and vision for design.

What do you see yourself doing after you finish this program?

Trying to expand my skills and start trying to involve myself in design, maybe even independently and start designing for myself, or finding something I can specialize in.

What excites you about design?

What worries you about design?

People not liking or understanding my artistic vision, in ways that make me feel lesser as a designer or being underwhelmed by the hard work I put in to making a cool design.

Can design change the world?

Yes, because it influences the perception of many people and gives them a new insight. People admire strong designs and they can be timeless pieces of work that defines cultures and artistic liberties.

What is a website?

It is a place on the web that presents information in a clear and concise manner, that anyone can access and gain new insight on just about anything that can or already exists.
